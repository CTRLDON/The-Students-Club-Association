From Your School to the Whole World.

Our model is designed to empower leaders at every level. Here’s the blueprint.

Level 1: The Global Council

The Student Club Association (SCA)

This is the head. SCA provides the global vision, the core funding, the technology, and the brand. We support the leaders of the Associations.

↓
Level 2: The Campus Leaders

School Student Association (SSA)

The leadership team for a specific school or area. They are trained by SCA to be the on-the-ground support system, managing and mentoring all clubs under them.

↓
Level 3: The Impact-Makers (You)

Your Club

This is your idea. Your passion. Your team. You are the reason SCA exists, getting full support from the entire network.
